Project details

Learn how to calculate attenuation in decibels and plot a Bode diagram to visualise attenuation as a function of frequency, enabling parameters such as bandwidth and cut-off frequency to be identified. Understand the importance of logarithmic scaling to effectively represent a wide range of frequencies and analyse the frequency behaviour of electronic systems. Finally, develop skills in experimental data processing and graphical visualisation using tools such as Excel and Python.
