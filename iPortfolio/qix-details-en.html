Project details

Development of the game logic using an object-oriented approach, facilitating the management of entities and their interactions. Creation of a clear, ergonomic user interface, incorporating interactive elements to enhance the gaming experience. Implementation of a collision management system, essential for detecting and reacting to player and enemy movements, with dynamic behaviours inspired by the strategic gameplay of QIX, where the player must conquer portions of the screen while avoiding unpredictable enemies.
